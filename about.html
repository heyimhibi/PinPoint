PinPoint what area works for you.


You have done your research and narrowed your search to the best universities and colleges for you future goals. Have you considered the type of city you are moving to? PinPoint is designed to help give those moving to a new city the ability to get a better idea of the area and what might be of interest to them.

PinPoint was designed by a collective of students call Game of Codes. Our goal is to make deciding to move to a new city a much simpler and enjoyable experience.
